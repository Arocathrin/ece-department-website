Alumni Engagement at ECE

Our graduates are essentially the backbone of the department, and we are proud of their leadership in private industries and government sectors. The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering links graduates with their alma mater.

Graduates contribute technically to the department as resource persons in seminars, workshops, alumnus lecture series, and faculty development programs by sharing their knowledge and skills. They also play a vital role in curriculum development by serving as members of the Board of Studies.

Opportunities are provided for students to connect with alumni during departmental visits, giving them valuable industrial exposure. Graduates benefit from reunions organized by the department, which help maintain professional networks and strengthen alumni-department relationships.
