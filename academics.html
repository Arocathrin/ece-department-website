Academic Programs

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at Karunya University offers a comprehensive range of academic programs designed to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge in the field of electronics and communication. The programs include:

  • B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • B.Tech. in Electronics and Computer Engineering (ECM)
  • M.Tech. in VLSI Design
  • Ph.D. in Electronics and Communication Engineering (Full-time and Part-time)

Curriculum Structure

The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in electronics and communication principles, along with hands-on experience in modern laboratories. Key areas of study include:

  • Digital Signal Processing
  • Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
  • VLSI Design
  • Communication Systems
  • Embedded Systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Students are encouraged to participate in research projects, internships, and industry collaborations to enhance their practical knowledge and employability.

Credit System

Karunya University follows the Choice Based Credit System (CBCS), offering flexibility for students to choose courses across different disciplines. This system allows students to tailor their education according to their interests and career goals, promoting interdisciplinary learning and innovation.
