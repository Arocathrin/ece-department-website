Department Library

The Department Library of Electronics and Communication Engineering is established to support the academic and research needs of students and faculty. It provides access to a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources in the field of electronics, communication, VLSI, embedded systems, IoT, and more.

The library is well-equipped with comfortable seating, computer terminals for digital access, and a peaceful environment for reading and research. Students can borrow textbooks, reference materials, and access e-resources through the OPAC system.
